# OSIRIS

**Open Source Intelligence Research & Investigation System**

OSIRIS is an AI-assisted Intelligence Runtime Environment designed to transform raw information into structured, evidence-based intelligence.

The objective of OSIRIS is not merely to collect information, but to support investigations through evidence management, entity resolution, knowledge representation, graph intelligence, reasoning, and decision support.

---

## Vision

OSIRIS exists to transform:

Information

↓

Evidence

↓

Knowledge

↓

Understanding

↓

Investigation

↓

Intelligence

↓

Decision Support

The system is designed for investigators, researchers, analysts, journalists, security professionals, and decision makers who require explainable and evidence-backed intelligence.

---

## What OSIRIS Is

OSIRIS is:

* An Intelligence Runtime Environment
* An Investigation Intelligence Platform
* A Knowledge Graph Intelligence System
* An Evidence-Centric Analysis Framework
* A Modular Intelligence Architecture
* A Foundation for Future Agent-Assisted Investigation

---

## What OSIRIS Is Not

OSIRIS is not:

* A chatbot
* A search engine
* A workflow automation script
* A data scraper
* A report generator
* A single AI agent
* An LLM wrapper

These capabilities may exist within OSIRIS, but they do not define the system.

---

## Core Principles

### Evidence Before Conclusions

Every intelligence conclusion should be traceable to evidence.

### Entity Identity Over Text

Reality is represented through entities.

Text is display.

Identity is intelligence.

### Explainable Intelligence

All conclusions should be auditable, reproducible, and understandable.

### Human Decision Authority

OSIRIS supports decision-making.

Humans remain responsible for final judgments.

### Continuous Improvement

Investigations generate knowledge.

Knowledge improves future investigations.
